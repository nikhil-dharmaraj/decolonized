DE-Colonized: The Stories That Matter

In the last three hundred years, the world has changed drastically, perhaps most notably from a world of colonial holdings to a world of independent nations. This transformation was not simple or straightforward, but gradual and complex. So often, colonization is taught and discussed in a way that solely highlights the deeds, battles, and events of the Western powers. And, thus, the stories, the people, the fighters behind the incredible de-colonization movements, are too often overlooked in this conversation.

This project aims to shed light on the stories that matter in the complex phenomenon that was the decolonization of the world: told not from the perspective of the colonizers, but from that of the revolutionaries.
